About

GDSC-CDGI is a teach-and-learn community focused on developer technologies, tools, and events powered by Google.This club is to help the students of CDGI to improve their technical and non-technical skills.Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. Currently GDSC is operational in more than 110+ countries and 1100 universities across the world.
